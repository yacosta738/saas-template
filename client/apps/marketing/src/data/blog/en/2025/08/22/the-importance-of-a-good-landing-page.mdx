A landing page is the first impression a potential customer has of your product. A good landing page can make the difference between a conversion and a bounce.

### Key Elements of a Good Landing Page

- **Compelling Headline:** Grab the user's attention with a clear and concise headline.
- **Clear Call to Action (CTA):** Tell the user what you want them to do next.
- **Engaging Visuals:** Use images and videos to showcase your product.
- **Social Proof:** Display testimonials and reviews from happy customers.
