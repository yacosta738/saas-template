## Overview

This is a generic privacy policy intended as a starting point for projects using this SaaS template. It is not legal advice. Please consult a lawyer to adapt this text for your product and jurisdiction.

## Information we collect

- Email addresses and names when users sign up for newsletters or waitlists.
- Usage data for analytics (IP addresses, device information, pages visited) using consented analytics tools.

## How we use data

- To communicate product updates and announcements.
- To improve the product and fix issues.
- To comply with legal obligations when required.

## Third parties

We may use third-party services (analytics, email delivery, authentication). Review the provider documentation and update this policy to list the specific services you use.

## Data retention

We retain user information only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. Implement retention and deletion processes appropriate for your product.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, adapt it, and add your contact email or data protection officer details.
